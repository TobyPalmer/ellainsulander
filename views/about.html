Hi, I'm Ella

A quick-witted visual designer and project manager from Gothenburg, with a passion for conceptual ideas. I’m currently studying my last year of the bachelor’s degree program GDK (Graphic Design & Communication) at Linköpings University in Norrköping, Sweden.

In all my work, digital as well as print, I’m striving for clarity and simplicity but with impact. I believe that good design is functional, simple and based on clever ideas and solutions. I am a versatile creator and team player that loves to learn new things and find new experiences.
